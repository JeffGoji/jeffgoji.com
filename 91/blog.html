First entry in the new blog! How exciting!
It took a while, but Miyoshi is now back on full daily-driver duties again.
I have to say, it feels a little weird adjusting from a huge crew-cab pickup back to my 91. Everything else on the road is positively huge, and visibility in traffic is pretty much non-existent.

And while I am happy to be back in a sports car for daily driving fun again, there are a few issues I’m going to have to work out. Like the AC is blowing hot again and just in time for summer… Oh joy. The 949 end-links have started to rattle on the front bar so those need to be replaced at some point. And the paint could use a good polish and a wax.

A Miata owner’s work is never done… Especially when it is a 30 year old Miata… Maybe I need to consider getting another daily driver.
